Odoo Slack Integration

Customer relationship management (CRM) manages your business's single most important goal - satisfying your customers. Slack is a proprietary business communication platform developed by American software company Slack Technologies. Dynexcel has developed an Odoo app which allows users to use the Slack Application to Send and Recieve messages via Odoo. We can Send messages, Recieve messages, Import Slack User To Odoo and Import Slack Channels. Let us have a look at how this works inside Odoo.

Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Odoo Enterprise (On-premise and Odoo.SH) as well as Community. Odoo Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Odoo Online.
