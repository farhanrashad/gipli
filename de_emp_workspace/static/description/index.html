Employee Self Service

Empower employees with seamless access to their personal records and the ability to take control of various essential tasks. With this user-friendly system, employees can effortlessly create and manage expenses, handle tasks, record attendance, and request time off, fostering independence and efficiency in their daily work.

Employee Detail

Access your complete employee profile and contract details conveniently. Gain insights into your employment history and contractual obligations.

Project & Tasks

Users will have access to their own projects and tasks, ensuring they can efficiently manage and track their individual workloads and project responsibilities.

Attendance

Access the Attendance and Kiosk Mode menus for streamlined time tracking and effortless employee check-ins.

Timeoff

Employees can conveniently view their time-off details, request time-off, and manage allocation with ease.

Expenses

Employees can effortlessly view their own expense details and submit new expense requests, simplifying the expense management process.
