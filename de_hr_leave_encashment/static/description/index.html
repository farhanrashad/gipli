ENCASHMENT

Employees can convert unused leave into cash

The Leave Encashment module enables employees to request and receive payments in exchange for their unused leave balances. It streamlines the process of converting accrued leave days into monetary compensation, providing employees with a flexible benefit while helping organizations manage their leave policies efficiently.

Leave Encashment Requests

Employees can easily submit leave encashment requests through a user-friendly interface. They can specify the number of leave days they want to encash and provide reasons for the request.

Automatic Leave Deduction

The module ensures that leave balances are automatically adjusted after the encashment request is approved, preventing any discrepancies in leave accounting.

Accurate Calculation

The module calculates the encashment amount based on employee-specific parameters, such as salary and entitlement, ensuring precise compensation.

Comprehensive Reporting

Administrators can access detailed reports to keep track of encashment activities within the context of the standard approval workflow. These reports facilitate auditing, analysis, and decision-making.

Leave Balances Visibility

Employees can easily view their leave balances, including encashed leaves, through their self-service portal, promoting transparency and empowerment.
